Platform 23

By Emperor Jack

An Atcs inspired map with some twists. For Unvanquished.

______________________________________________________________________________________________

Backstory :A ship platform on one of Humanity's many planets. Provides fuel and resource for incoming ships. Alien infestation spotted and a quarentine put in place. Human team teleported in to exterminate threat. Aliens wish to cut off supplies to ships in numerous areas on the planet and have attacked this platform.

2vs2 or more
3vs3 and 4vs4 most applicable

Map/Compile information:

Brush count: 3144

-meta -custinfoparms -keeplights -samplesize 8											: 7 Seconds
-vis -saveprt															: 2 Seconds
-light -fast -shade -dirty -patchshadows -deluxe -deluxemode 0 -lightmapsize 512 -samples 3 -samplesize 8 -bouncegrid -bounce 6	: 413 Seconds
